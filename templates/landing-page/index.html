Spark your Big Data

RAD (RAD Analytics Distribution) is a Red Hat sponsored distribution of open source analytics tools that form the core of a next generation analytics platform. It is curated with tight integration between its components, a predictable release cadence, published roadmap and defined community support policy. Included technologies fit architecturally, address a relevant use case, and have minimal external dependencies. It is also a user focused community to develop technology in a use case driven fashion.
